# Account Deletion Instructions

You can delete your RoundEvolution game account and associated data in the following two ways.

## Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

This is the fastest method, and you can do it directly within the game:

1. Open the **RoundEvolution** application.
2. Tap the **Settings** button in the bottom right corner of the main screen.
3. Switch to the **Account** tab.
4. Tap the red **Delete Account** button.
5. Read the warning message in the confirmation window and confirm the deletion.

> **Note**: This action will permanently delete all your game progress, purchase history, and account data. This action cannot be undone.

## Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to log in to the application, you can also contact us via email to request a manual deletion:

Please send an email to: `ha516Studio@gmail.com`

**Email Template**:

*   **Subject**: Request to delete account
*   **Body**:

    ```text
    Dear RoundEvolution Support,

    I would like to request the deletion of my account and all associated data.

    User ID: [Your User ID]

    I understand that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

    Sincerely,
    [Your Name]
    ```

We will assist you with the account deletion process within 7 business days of receiving your request.

# 帳號刪除說明 (Account Deletion)

您可以使用以下兩種方式刪除您的 RoundEvolution 遊戲帳號與相關資料。

## 方式一：於應用程式內刪除 (推薦)

這是最快速的方式，您可以直接在遊戲中自行操作：

1. 開啟 **RoundEvolution** 應用程式。
2. 點擊主畫面右下角的 **Settings (設定)**。
3. 切換至 **Account (帳號)** 分頁。
4. 點擊紅色的 **Delete Account (刪除帳號)** 按鈕。
5. 閱讀確認視窗的警示訊息，並確認刪除。

> **注意**：此操作將永久刪除您的所有遊戲進度、購買紀錄與帳號資料，且無法復原。

## 方式二：透過電子郵件申請人工刪除

若您無法登入應用程式，也可以透過電子郵件聯繫我們進行人工刪除：

請發送郵件至：`ha516Studio@gmail.com`

**郵件範例 (Email Template)**：

*   **標題 (Subject)**: Request to delete account
*   **內容 (Body)**:

    ```text
    Dear RoundEvolution Support,

    I would like to request the deletion of my account and all associated data.

    User ID: [您的使用者 ID]

    I understand that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

    Sincerely,
    [您的名字]
    ```

我們會於收到請求後的 7 個工作天內協助您完成帳號刪除作業。
