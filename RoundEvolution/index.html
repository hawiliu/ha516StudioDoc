About Round Evolution

Developed by ha516Studio, Round Evolution is a strategic puzzle game where players merge spheres to evolve them into higher forms. Reflecting our commitment to clean design and smooth gameplay, this game challenges your reflexes and planning skills in an engaging arcade experience.

Data Usage Transparency:
To provide a seamless experience, improve our services, and offer personalized content, the Round Evolution application may collect limited user data, including:

  • Device Information & IP Address: Used for analytics and app performance monitoring.
  • App Activity: Used to understand user engagement and improve game features.
  • Advertising IDs: Used by third-party services (AdMob) to deliver relevant advertisements.

We value your privacy. All data collection is conducted in accordance with our Privacy Policy and is never sold to third parties.
